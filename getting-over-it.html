OVER THE MOUNTAIN

a game about climbing

You are a man in a pot, armed with one heavy hammer.

Move the mouse to swing the hammer. Push off the rocks and hook the hammer over ledges to haul your whole body upward.

There are no checkpoints. If you slip, you fall — and you lose everything you climbed. That is the point.
