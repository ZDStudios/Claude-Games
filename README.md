## 🕹️ Games

### 🐠 Reefkeeper — *A Cozy Aquarium*

> *Grow your tank. Discover rare variants. Rule the reef.*

A laid-back aquarium idle game where you raise fish, unlock new species, and hunt for elusive rare colour variants.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| 🎮 Genre | Idle / Clicker |
| 🐡 Species | 24 — fish, crabs, seahorses, sharks, jellyfish, octopuses, stingrays & turtles |
| ✨ Variants | Silver ✦ · Gold ★ · Rainbow ✺ (up to **40×** value) |
| 📱 Platform | Browser · Mobile-friendly |

**How to play:**
- 🖱️ Click ripe fish to **sell** them
- 💧 Click empty water to **drop food** — fish swim to it for a growth boost
- 🌊 Watch for **WILD!** fish that swim through — tap to catch them free
- 🥚 Breed same-species fish for a 5× better shot at rare variants
- 🛒 Spend earnings in the Shop on new species & upgrades
